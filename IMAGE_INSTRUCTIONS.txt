IMPORTANT: Please manually add the SP500 stock prediction image

1. Save the stock market chart image (the one with green/red candlesticks and numbers) as:
   sp500-stock-prediction.jpg

2. Place it in this folder:
   src\assets\

3. The file should be named exactly: sp500-stock-prediction.jpg

After adding the image, the project will display correctly with the SP500 Stock Price Prediction project at the top of your projects section.
